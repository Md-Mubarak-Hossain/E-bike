Tulip tours

Welcome Spring by cycling through the Dutch Tulip Fields

Tulip tours

Our bike tours (and bike and boat tours) in Holland during the tulip season are among our most popular tours.

Escape the winter drab on a bike in the exploding colors of spring! From the end of March to mid-May (weather dependent), the Dutch landscape is transformed. The fields awake in brilliance with the blooming of crocuses, daffodils, hyacinths, and the national flower of Holland, the tulip.

The Keukenhof, the largest flower garden in the world, opens its gates, revealing the splendor of more than 7 million flowers in bloom.

Plan your Tulip Tour escape now!
